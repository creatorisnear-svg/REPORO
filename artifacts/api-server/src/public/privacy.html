Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who We Are

Aviv Bot ("we", "us", "our") is a Discord bot and subscription service designed for Rust Console Edition server communities. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the bot's features:

  • Discord user ID and username - used to link your Discord account to your in-game name and manage your economy balance, warnings, and other per-user data.
  • In-game name (Gamertag) - entered by you when using /link. Used to identify you across bot features.
  • Discord server (guild) ID - used to scope all data to your Discord server.
  • Economy and game data - coin balances, kit cooldowns, gambling history, warning records, prison records, ZORP zones, raid alert frequencies, and similar game state data generated by using bot features.
  • RCON connection details - host, port, and password for your Rust server. Stored encrypted in our database and used only to maintain your RCON connection.
  • Payment information - handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store card numbers or payment details. We store only your Stripe customer ID and subscription status.
  • Session data - if you log in through the dashboard via Discord OAuth, we store a temporary session cookie to keep you logged in.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver the features you use (economy, kits, shop, gambling, moderation, etc.)
  • To maintain your RCON connection to your Rust server
  • To process subscription payments through Stripe
  • To authenticate you on the dashboard
  • To send you in-Discord notifications (raid alerts, prison DMs, etc.) that you have opted into

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your subscription is active or your Discord server has the bot installed. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon cancellation, data is retained for 30 days and then deleted unless you request earlier removal.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Discord - We interact with Discord's API to operate the bot. Discord's own Privacy Policy applies to your Discord account.
  • Stripe - Payment processing. Your payment data is handled by Stripe under their Privacy Policy.
  • Turso (libSQL) - Database hosting. Data is stored in a managed SQLite database hosted by Turso.
  • Koyeb - Server hosting. The bot and web server run on Koyeb infrastructure.

6. Cookies

We use a single session cookie when you log in through the dashboard. This cookie is used only to maintain your login session and expires when you log out or after 7 days. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR and CCPA) including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our Discord support server.

8. Children

Aviv Bot is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. RCON passwords are stored with encryption. However, no internet service is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Aviv Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests, join our Discord support server or contact us through the bot's dashboard.
